Banitsa

Greatest bulgarian dish!

You can make it by yourself or by it from a bakery

We'll show you how to prepare it!

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Flour

Steps:

  1. Put some cheese in a bowl
  2. Add salt and oil
  3. Stir with the flour
  4. Put all things together and bake it for 1hr in the oven
